What is Cyber Security?

Cyber security is how individuals and organisations reduce the risk of cyber attack. Cyber security can be described as the collective methods, technologies, and processes to help protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of computer systems, networks and data, against cyber-attacks or unauthorized access.

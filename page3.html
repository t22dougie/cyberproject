Forms Of Cyber Attacks

Denial of Service Attacks

Denial of service attacks can be genuine when a black friday deals gets released for example and many users rush to cash in and the website cannot handle the demand. On the other hand denial of service attacks can be launched by an attacker to take down a website by using all the resources to prevent any access to the website, this is a common tactic of Russian intelligence agencies for attacking their desired target.

Man in the Middle Attacks

A man in the middle attack is when the attacker places themselves between the user and the system or between users and intercepts messages and communication, this is done to gain personal information or disrupt communication between the user and their intended recipient. This can be prevented with end-to-end encryption meaning the information the attacker would recieve is encrypted and therefore unreadable.

Phishing Attacks

Phishing attacks are when an attacker will email victims with a message of urgency to attempt to persuade them to click on a malicious link which often takes them to a fake website which will try to gain personal information or financial information of the victim to use for their own means.
